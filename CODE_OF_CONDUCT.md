# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus on technical accuracy, not tribal loyalty to specific labs or models
- Base claims on benchmarks and evidence, not hype
- Welcome corrections — outdated or wrong information is a bug

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Vendor shilling or undisclosed promotional content
- Dismissing contributions without explanation
- Harassment of any kind

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who repeatedly violate these standards.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, and discussions.
